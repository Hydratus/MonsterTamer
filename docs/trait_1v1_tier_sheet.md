## Reading Guide

- S: immediate watchlist; likely overtuned or very flexible in 1v1.
- A: strong and broadly draftable trait cores.
- B: generally fair; often build-dependent or matchup-dependent.
- C: niche, high-risk, or currently under-tuned for 1v1 consistency.

## Notes

- Current traits receive a small reliability bonus; future traits are scored more conservatively.
- Raw damage, sustain efficiency, stat amplification, and consistency gates weigh heavily.
- Weird high-variance mechanics can still be excellent in play despite lower heuristic scores.
